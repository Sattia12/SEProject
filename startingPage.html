About the application

The application will be utilised by trainees who take the online exams for the completion of a specific pathway and by assessors, which will be responsible for formulating the questions and utilise the results to overview the progress of trainees during the training period.

The application will provide great flexibility to the assessors, such as deciding the exam format (multiple-choice, quiz-based, open-ended, true or false), adding multimedia, deciding the length of an exam and tracking progress over time.

The system will also record the progress of students, generating a report of the overall performance of every student, the average grade in that exam, the percentage of students who failed/passed, and accurate feedback on a traineeâ€™s progress. This automatization leads to less human-prone errors in the process of analysing results.
